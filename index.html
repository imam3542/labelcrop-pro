What is LabelCrop Pro?

LabelCrop Pro is a professional browser-based automation tool for marketplace sellers. If you sell on Meesho or Flipkart, you know the struggle — every order comes as a multi-page PDF containing invoices, terms, and the actual shipping label. Manually extracting hundreds of labels is tedious and error-prone.

Our tool solves this. You upload your PDFs, and our intelligent cropping engine automatically detects and extracts only the shipping label. No server uploads means your customer data stays private. No software installation required.

Whether you process 10 orders or 1000 orders daily, LabelCrop Pro saves hours of manual work. Join thousands of sellers who have automated their label extraction workflow.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Meesho Label Cropper?

A Meesho Label Cropper automatically extracts shipping labels from Meesho invoice PDFs, removing invoices and creating clean, print-ready labels for all courier partners.

How does Flipkart PDF Cropper work?

Flipkart PDF Cropper uses smart detection to locate AWB labels within Flipkart PDFs, strips away invoice pages, and exports labels in bulk ZIP format.

Is there a free Meesho Excel Report Generator?

Yes. LabelCrop Pro includes a free Excel report generator that extracts order IDs, AWB numbers, customer names, and addresses from Meesho PDFs.

Is my data uploaded to any server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your computer.

Can I process multiple PDFs at once?

Yes. LabelCrop Pro supports batch processing of up to 100 PDFs per session with ZIP export for all extracted labels.

Which marketplaces are supported?

Currently Meesho and Flipkart. More platforms coming soon.
